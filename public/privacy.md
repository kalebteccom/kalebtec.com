Last updated: March 2026

## Introduction

Kalebtec ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit kalebtec.com ("the Site").

## Information We Collect

When you use the Site, we may collect the following types of information:

### Information You Provide

- **Contact form submissions**: Name, email address, and message content when you submit our contact form
- **Email correspondence**: Information you provide when contacting us directly at hello@kalebtec.com

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage data**: Anonymous analytics data about how you interact with the Site, including pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns
- **Technical data**: Browser type, operating system, device information, and IP address for security and performance purposes
- **Theme preferences**: Your selected display theme (dark/light), stored locally in your browser

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Respond to inquiries and messages submitted through the contact form
- Improve the Site's performance, accessibility, and user experience
- Protect the security and integrity of the Site
- Communicate about our consulting services when you have requested information
- Analyze usage patterns to improve our content and services

## Data Sharing

Kalebtec does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

- **Service providers**: With trusted providers who assist in operating the Site (e.g., hosting, analytics), bound by confidentiality obligations
- **Legal requirements**: When required by law, regulation, or legal process, or to protect our legal rights
- **With your consent**: When you have given explicit permission to share your information

## Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Cookies and Local Storage

The Site uses minimal cookies and local storage for essential functionality:

- **Theme preference**: Your selected display theme is stored in your browser's localStorage
- **Essential cookies**: Used for basic Site functionality only

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking cookies.

## Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and correspondence for as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and maintain our business records. Anonymous usage data may be retained in aggregate form indefinitely.

## Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access**: Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal information
- **Opt-out**: Opt out of non-essential data collection
- **Portability**: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@kalebtec.com.

## Children's Privacy

The Site is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

## International Users

The Site is operated remotely and may be accessed from various countries. By using the Site, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.
